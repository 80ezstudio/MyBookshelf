Delete your My Bookshelf account

This page explains how to request deletion of your My Bookshelf account and associated app data.

How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your My Bookshelf account, email us using the address below:

Request account deletion

Please send the request from the email address linked to your My Bookshelf account, or include the account email address in your message.

What data will be deleted

When you request deletion, we will delete your My Bookshelf account and associated app data linked to that account, including reading records, saved book progress, notes, quotes and ratings where this data is stored with your account.

What data may be retained

We may retain limited information only where required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or operational reasons. Any retained information will be kept only for as long as necessary.

Processing time

We aim to process account deletion requests within 30 days.
