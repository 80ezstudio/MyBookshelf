My Bookshelf Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how 80EZ Studio handles information when you use My Bookshelf.

My Bookshelf is a personal reading tracker app. It is designed to help you save books, track reading progress, manage notes and quotes, organise your personal bookshelf, and, where available, use account, login, sync, backup, paid, or premium features.

Who We Are

My Bookshelf is developed and published by 80EZ Studio.

Contact email: 80ezstudio@gmail.com

Information We Collect

My Bookshelf may collect, store, or process information you provide or create in the app so that the app can operate and provide its features.

This may include:

  • Books you add to your bookshelf
  • Book titles, authors, page counts, cover information, descriptions, and related book details
  • Reading status, reading progress, ratings, reading dates, favourites, and reading history
  • Notes, quotes, and other reading-related text you choose to save
  • Profile preferences, such as display name, reading bio, and selected preset avatar
  • App settings and preferences
  • Account-related information, where account features are available, such as email address, user ID, login provider, authentication status, and account settings
  • Purchase or entitlement information, where paid features are available, such as whether your account or device has access to a paid feature, subscription, trial, or premium entitlement
  • Technical information needed to operate, secure, diagnose, and improve the app, such as app version, device information, crash or error information, and service logs where these features are enabled

Account, Login, and Firebase Services

My Bookshelf may use Firebase services provided by Google to support account login, authentication, app security, diagnostics, cloud-based features, or future sync and backup functionality.

If you create or use an account, Firebase or related service providers may process account and technical information needed to provide the service. This may include your email address, user identifier, authentication provider, authentication tokens, device or app information, and technical logs.

Firebase and Google services are provided by Google and may process information in accordance with their own privacy and security practices. We use these services to operate My Bookshelf features, manage accounts, maintain security, and support app functionality.

Local and Cloud Storage

Some My Bookshelf data may be stored locally on your device. This may include your books, reading progress, notes, quotes, preferences, and other app information.

Where account, login, sync, backup, cloud, or paid features are enabled, some information may also be stored or processed using Firebase, Google services, or other trusted service providers so that the app can provide those features.

This means that not all app data is necessarily stored only on your device once account, cloud, sync, backup, or paid features are enabled.

Book Search and Book Information

My Bookshelf may use external book information sources to help search for books and display book details such as title, author, description, cover image, page count, and identifiers such as ISBN.

When you search for a book, the search terms, ISBN, or related search information you enter may be sent to the relevant book information service so search results can be returned. These services are not controlled by 80EZ Studio and may process data according to their own privacy policies.

My Bookshelf does not sell your saved reading notes, personal bookshelf, ratings, or reading progress.

Paid Features, Purchases, and Subscriptions

My Bookshelf may offer paid features, subscriptions, one-time purchases, premium access, trials, or other paid products now or in the future.

Payments and purchase management may be handled by Google Play or another authorised payment provider. We do not directly receive or store your full payment card details.

We may receive or process information needed to confirm, unlock, manage, or restore paid access. This may include purchase status, subscription status, entitlement status, product identifiers, transaction identifiers, account identifiers, and related technical information.

Paid features, prices, subscription options, included features, trial availability, billing periods, and access rules may change over time, subject to applicable law and app store rules.

How We Use Information

We may use information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain My Bookshelf
  • Save and display your personal bookshelf
  • Track your reading progress
  • Save notes, quotes, ratings, preferences, and reading information
  • Provide account login, authentication, account management, and security features
  • Provide cloud, sync, backup, or restore features where available
  • Provide, manage, restore, or verify paid features, subscriptions, trials, or premium access
  • Respond to support requests
  • Diagnose technical issues, crashes, errors, and performance problems
  • Prevent misuse, fraud, abuse, or security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms
  • Improve the app and develop new features

Permissions

My Bookshelf may request permissions such as camera access for features like barcode scanning.

Permissions are used only for the features you choose to use. You can manage app permissions through your device settings.

Profile Avatars and Images

My Bookshelf may offer preset avatar options for profiles. If custom image upload features are added in the future, this Privacy Policy may be updated to explain how those images are handled.

Analytics, Advertising, Crash Reporting, and Tracking

My Bookshelf does not currently sell personal information for advertising.

My Bookshelf may use technical services, including Firebase or Google services, to support authentication, diagnostics, crash reporting, security, app performance, or future analytics where enabled.

If advertising, personalised ads, or additional analytics services are introduced in the future, this Privacy Policy and the Google Play Data Safety information will be updated where required before those services are used in a released version of the app.

Data Sharing

80EZ Studio does not sell your personal information.

We may share or process information with trusted service providers only where reasonably needed to operate My Bookshelf, provide authentication, manage accounts, process or verify purchases, provide app features, maintain security, diagnose technical issues, comply with legal obligations, or protect our rights and users.

These service providers may include Firebase, Google services, Google Play, payment or purchase providers, book information services, hosting providers, support tools, or other technical providers used to operate the app.

Data Retention and Deletion

Information stored locally on your device remains on your device until you delete it in the app, clear the app’s data, or uninstall the app.

Information stored or processed through account, cloud, sync, backup, purchase, or service-provider features may be retained for as long as reasonably needed to provide the app, maintain your account, verify purchases, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, maintain security, and enforce our terms.

You may delete local app data by deleting information within the app where controls are available, clearing the app’s data, or uninstalling the app.

If account or cloud features are available, you may contact us to request account deletion or cloud data deletion, subject to legal, security, fraud-prevention, purchase, backup, and technical limitations.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to help protect information. However, no method of transmission, processing, or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

You are responsible for keeping your device and account secure, including using your device’s screen lock, protecting your login credentials, keeping your operating system updated, and using secure backup settings where appropriate.

Children’s Privacy

My Bookshelf is intended as a general audience app and is not specifically directed to children under 13.

80EZ Studio does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through My Bookshelf. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

Your Choices

You may choose whether to use optional account, login, sync, backup, cloud, or paid features where those features are available.

You can delete books, notes, quotes, ratings, and other saved information within the app where those controls are provided.

You can manage device permissions through your device settings.

You can stop using My Bookshelf by uninstalling it from your device.

You can manage subscriptions or purchases through Google Play or the relevant payment provider where applicable.

Future Features and Changes

My Bookshelf may add, remove, change, limit, suspend, or discontinue features over time. This may include free features, paid features, account features, sync, backup, recommendations, premium access, subscriptions, trials, or other services.

We may also change pricing, subscription options, included features, limits, eligibility, or access rules, subject to applicable law and app store rules.

If future features materially change how we collect, use, store, or share information, this Privacy Policy will be updated where required.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of My Bookshelf after changes take effect means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, My Bookshelf, or data requests, contact:

Email: 80ezstudio@gmail.com
