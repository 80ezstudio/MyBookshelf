My Bookshelf Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how 80EZ Studio handles information when you use My Bookshelf.

My Bookshelf is a personal reading tracker app. It is designed to help you save books, track reading progress, manage notes and quotes, and organise your personal bookshelf on your device.

Who We Are

My Bookshelf is developed and published by 80EZ Studio.

Contact email: 80ezstudio@gmail.com

Current Account Status

My Bookshelf does not currently provide a live online account system.

Account-related screens may appear in the app as part of a coming-soon account flow, but real account login, email verification, cloud sync, and online account services are not currently active.

My Bookshelf does not currently send account verification emails, create online user accounts, or sync your bookshelf to 80EZ Studio servers.

Information Stored on Your Device

My Bookshelf stores app information locally on your device so the app can work as a personal bookshelf and reading tracker.

This may include:

  • Books you add to your bookshelf
  • Book titles, authors, page counts, cover information, descriptions, and related book details
  • Reading status, progress, ratings, reading dates, and favourites
  • Notes, quotes, and other reading-related text you choose to save
  • Profile preferences, such as display name, reading bio, and selected preset avatar
  • App settings and preferences

This information is stored locally on your device. 80EZ Studio does not currently collect, receive, or store this information on its own servers.

Information We Do Not Currently Collect

My Bookshelf does not currently collect or send the following information to 80EZ Studio:

  • Your email address for a live account
  • Your password
  • Your phone number
  • Your precise location
  • Your contacts or messages
  • Your personal photos for profile images
  • Your manually uploaded book photos
  • Your payment details
  • Your reading data for cloud sync

Profile Avatars and Images

My Bookshelf uses preset avatar options for profiles. The app does not currently allow users to upload custom profile photos.

The app does not currently allow users to upload custom book photos for public or online use.

Book Search and Book Information

My Bookshelf may use external book information sources to help search for books and display book details such as title, author, description, cover image, page count, and identifiers such as ISBN.

When you search for a book, the search terms or ISBN you enter may be sent to the relevant book information service so search results can be returned. These services are not controlled by 80EZ Studio and may process data according to their own privacy policies.

My Bookshelf does not currently send your saved reading notes, personal bookshelf, ratings, or reading progress to 80EZ Studio servers.

How We Use Information

Information stored in the app is used to provide the app’s features, including:

  • Saving and displaying your personal bookshelf
  • Tracking your reading progress
  • Saving notes and quotes
  • Showing reading statistics
  • Saving your app preferences and profile settings

Because My Bookshelf does not currently provide cloud sync or live online accounts, this information is intended to remain on your device unless you choose to share it outside the app using device or system features.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

My Bookshelf does not currently use third-party advertising services.

My Bookshelf does not currently use third-party analytics or tracking services to collect personal data for advertising or profiling.

If analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or similar services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy and the Google Play Data Safety information will be updated before those services are used in a released version of the app.

Data Sharing

80EZ Studio does not currently sell your personal information.

80EZ Studio does not currently share your personal bookshelf, notes, quotes, profile preferences, or reading progress with third parties.

Book search requests may be sent to external book information services only as needed to provide book search and book detail features.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because My Bookshelf currently stores your bookshelf and app data locally on your device, this data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, clear the app’s data, or uninstall the app.

Uninstalling the app or clearing app data may delete locally stored bookshelf information, notes, progress, and preferences from your device.

Because My Bookshelf does not currently provide live online accounts, 80EZ Studio does not currently hold account data on its servers for you to access, correct, or delete.

Security

My Bookshelf is designed to keep your reading data on your device in the current version.

You are responsible for keeping your device secure, including using your device’s screen lock, operating system security updates, and backup settings where appropriate.

Children’s Privacy

My Bookshelf is intended as a general audience app and is not specifically directed to children.

80EZ Studio does not knowingly collect personal information from children through My Bookshelf.

Future Features

Future versions of My Bookshelf may include optional Pro features, account login, account sync, cloud backup, recommendations, or other online features.

If those features are introduced, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features collect, sync, or process additional personal data.

Your Choices

You can use My Bookshelf without a live online account in the current version.

You can delete books, notes, and other saved information within the app where those controls are provided.

You can also stop using My Bookshelf by uninstalling it from your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
